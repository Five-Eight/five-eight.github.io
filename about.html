FiveEight is a digital tool that seeks to help clients understand how to use different financial resources, and enable access to the right ones. The platform combines lessons in financial literacy with information on relevant financial products, in order to assist clients in reaching a state of financial wellness.

Financial wellness enables you to manage your personal finances in such a way that informs your decisions and helps empower the pursuit of your goals.

By delivering accurate, understandable, and intentional content, the FiveEight curriculum demystifies today’s confusing, and often intimidating, financial landscape. We provide clients with a roadmap toward financial wellness, helping them achieve a feeling of financial stability and assuredness, through all of life’s ups and downs.

Unlike many providers of financial products, FiveEight seeks to empower our clients, not exploit them.

That is why our innovative model doesn’t just provide access to resources, but importantly combines that access with information on how to leverage such resources to their greatest advantage.

We seek to meet clients where they are in their financial journey, and enable them to overcome their particular financial challenges to reach their individual goals. We realize that the last thing clients need is more stress about their financial situation, thus the FiveEight process and environment is a non-judgemental, safe space where clients can learn and become more confident in their financial literacy, health, and position.

As a company, we believe that:

  • Everyone deserves to feel secure and empowered by their financial situation; if they don’t, we’re here to help change that.

  • Information and financial literacy is essential to people’s ability to understand, address, and improve their financial position.

  • Having access to financial resources, and knowing how to leverage them, is critical to maximizing financial security and success.

  • Technology can enable financial clarity and provide access to key products and resources.

We value:

Equality and Inclusivity: Historically, financial literacy has been an advantage that has benefited certain groups of society and allowed them to succeed over others. FiveEight seeks to put an egalitarian lens on financial access, enabling more people to understand how to better position their personal finances.

Impact and Outcomes: The most critical aspect of the FiveEight process is that it is successful in helping our clients achieve a state of financial wellness. Thus, we track outcomes closely and report on them transparently so we can continuously improve our curriculum and resources.

Integrity: We prioritize our clients, their experience, and their financial health over all else. We would never provide or recommend a financial resource that we wouldn’t use ourselves or sacrifice client outcomes for economic gain. We are passionate about financial wellness, and promoting it is at the core of every choice FiveEight makes as a company.
