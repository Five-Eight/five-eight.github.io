Our mission

Decisions

We make thousands of decisions per day, and those we make about personal finances are ill-informed, even if money doesn’t enter or leave your account … the decisions are still made without the right information. Every ill-informed decision on your personal finances has a cost. FiveEight is here to ensure that every decision you make about your personal finances is an informed decision.

Innovation

FiveEight is exclusively about Financial Wellness, which we define as the balance between having the right information about how and when to use financial products (financial literacy) and access to those products. We identify innovative ways for the pressing concerns in your personal finances; debt reduction, increasing savings and building wealth are amongst the first areas FiveEight targets.

Eliminate Predatory Lending

40% of us can’t come up with £400 in case of an emergency and 78% of us live paycheck to paycheck. When times are tough and you don’t have a plan, there are few places to turn to. Money has always been easy to come by, it’s the cost of money that troubles us. The predatory lending market is valued in the billions and we’re here to make sure you don’t fall victim to it.
