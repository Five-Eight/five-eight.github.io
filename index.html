Our products

We deliver weekly learning related to personal finances via an email digest, supported by an online portal with tools and templates to put knowledge into practice.

Tools & Templates

Employee portal with online content, tools, templates and planners including quizzes to assist the user to commit content to memory and create new, healthier financial habits

5-8 Digest

Weekly email digest of financial tips and processes, relevant news, opinion pieces, user stories and updates on new online tools. Includes a separate weekly reminder/motivational note

Online Workshops

Monthly, small group sessions (limited to those in a single company) to share concerns, hear from others and clarify ambiguities. Sessions are voluntary, structured and confidential
